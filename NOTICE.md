## Third-Party Content

This project's `database/` directory contains curated parameter tables, algorithm
cards, and teaching guides derived from publicly available NIST publications and
academic papers. The `official/` directory contains PDF files that are the
property of their respective publishers and are included for educational and
research reference purposes only.

- NIST Internal Reports (IR series) — National Institute of Standards and Technology
